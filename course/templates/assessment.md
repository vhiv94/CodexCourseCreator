# Assessment template

Use this template for:

- a pre-structure assessment before deciding whether the course should use `chapters` or `modules`
- a prechapter assessment before writing any lessons for a chapter

Keep the format short-answer and learner-friendly. Short bullet answers are fine.

## How to answer

- Answer in your own words.
- Short bullet answers are fine.
- If you feel unsure, say what you think and what still feels fuzzy.
- Use examples from tools, apps, or workflows you already know when helpful.

## Prompts

1. What feels most familiar here already?
2. What feels least familiar or most uncertain?
3. In plain language, how would you explain the core concept for this phase?
4. What mistakes do you think would be easy to make here?
5. What would you want extra review or examples for?

## Routing notes

- Use the answers to tune scope, pacing, and review depth.
- Do not use the assessment to skip whole foundations automatically.
- For prechapter assessments, keep logical lesson ids unchanged and use the assessment only to personalize the upcoming batch.
